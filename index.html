ISO Control Gates
CG-01 Data Complete?No WO if design ACC, deadline, specs, or instruction is incomplete.
CG-02 Material Ready?No production start before material is available and checked.
CG-03 Pre-Production OK?Machine, WO, blueprint, and materials must match before execution.
CG-05 Lolos QC?No product enters ready-to-ship without QC pass and defect decision.
